Recipe for Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoon yeast
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk

For the filling

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablesspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with yeast, sugar and eggs
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
